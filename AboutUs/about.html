Vision

Promotion of psychosocial and mental health well-being for all.

Mission

To advance psychosocial and mental health well-being for all individuals through comprehensive promotion and support initiatives.

Goal

To cultivate an environment where psychosocial and mental health are prioritized, ensuring access to resources, education, and support services that empower individuals to thrive emotionally and psychologically.

Strengths

  • Specialized expertise in mental health, personal development, and organizational consulting.
  • Strong reputation and trust within Pokhara for high-quality services.
  • Client-centric approach with a focus on understanding and meeting client needs.
  • Innovative and effective solutions to clients' challenges.
  • Collaborative partnerships with other organizations and professionals in Pokhara.

Key Features

What We Do and For Whom

  • Provide psychological counseling services for individuals with various psychosocial and mental health concerns.
  • Lead and facilitate both long-term and short-term capacity-building training in psychosocial and mental health areas.
  • Conduct research in the fields of psychosocial and mental health.
  • Support mental health treatment and improve understanding of mental health and psychosocial aspects.

For Whom?

We work with individuals experiencing psychosocial and mental health challenges, their families, college students, and community key figures.

Our Major Services

  • Individual/family counseling (home-based care if needed)
  • Mental health treatment and referral for rehabilitation care
  • 780-hour training course on psychosocial counseling (approved by the National Health Training Centre - NHTC)
  • Short training sessions on stress management, anger management, self-care, parenting, and motivation.
  • Scientific research and studies.
